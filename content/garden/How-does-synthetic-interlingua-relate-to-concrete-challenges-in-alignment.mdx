# How does synthetic interlingua relate to concrete challenges in alignment?

Synthetic interlingua is a way of improving the interpretability process by both making models more cognitively ergonomic for humans and making people better at interpreting them. In this, it aims to improve oversight schemes implemented by both humans and auxiliary models (i.e. scalable mechanistic interpretability).
